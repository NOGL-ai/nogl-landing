# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### How often is competitor data refreshed?

By default, competitor prices are scraped daily (midnight UTC). Growth and Enterprise plans get 6-hour and 1-hour refresh cycles respectively. You can also trigger an on-demand refresh from the competitor profile page.

### Can I track prices on Amazon and other marketplaces?

Yes. Add the marketplace URL or specific product listing URL in **Companies → Add Competitor**. NOGL will attempt to track that listing. Note that some marketplaces use dynamic pricing that changes by minute; NOGL captures a snapshot at each scrape time.

### Is there an API?

Yes. The NOGL REST API is available on Growth and Enterprise plans. Documentation is available at `/api/docs` in your workspace.

## Pricing & Billing

### What counts as a "tracked product"?

A tracked product is any SKU in your catalog with **Status: Active** that has at least one competitor match. Inactive and draft products do not count toward your plan limit.

### Can I downgrade my plan?

Yes, at the end of your billing cycle. If you have more tracked products than the lower plan allows, you'll need to deactivate some products first.

## Repricing

### What happens if no competitor data is available for a rule?

If a repricing rule cannot find competitor data for a product, it **does not change the price**. The rule logs a "no competitor data" event in the repricing history.

### Can a rule change my price multiple times per day?

Only if the competitor price changes multiple times per day and your plan includes sub-daily scrapes. On the Starter plan (daily scrapes), each rule can trigger at most once per 24 hours per product.

## Data & Security

### Where is my data stored?

All data is stored in the EU (Frankfurt, Germany). NOGL is GDPR-compliant.

### Can I delete my data?

Yes. Go to **Settings → Workspace → Data Retention** and request a full data export and deletion. Account deletion is processed within 30 days.
