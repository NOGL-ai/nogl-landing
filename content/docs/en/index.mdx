# Fractional CFO Overview

Welcome to the NOGL Fractional CFO help center. This documentation covers everything you need to manage pricing, track competitors, and optimize your e-commerce margins.

## What's covered

- **Forecasting** — Demand and revenue projections based on historical data
- **Replenishment** — Inventory reorder recommendations driven by sell-through rates
- **Repricing** — Automated rules to keep your prices competitive
- **Demand Shaping** — Tools to influence purchase timing and volume
- **Competitor Tracking** — Monitor rival prices in real time
- **Alerts** — Configurable notifications for price changes, stock levels, and more
- **Assets** — Manage product images, descriptions, and feed content

## Who this is for

These docs are written for operations and support teams who work with the platform daily. No coding knowledge is required.
