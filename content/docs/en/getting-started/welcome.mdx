# Welcome to NOGL

NOGL is an AI-powered pricing intelligence platform that helps e-commerce teams track competitors, automate repricing, and forecast demand.

## Your first login

1. Navigate to your workspace URL (e.g. `https://app.nogl.ai/en`).
2. Sign in with your company email and password.
3. You'll land on the **Dashboard**, which shows key metrics at a glance.

## Dashboard overview

The dashboard displays:

- **Revenue trend** — Rolling 30-day revenue vs the previous period
- **Margin health** — Average margin across your active catalog
- **Top movers** — Products with the largest price changes in the last 7 days
- **Alerts** — Unread notifications requiring your attention

## Navigation

The left sidebar groups features into sections:

| Section | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| Catalog | View and edit your product catalog |
| Companies | Track competitor companies and their pricing |
| Repricing | Configure and review auto-repricing rules |
| Reports | Export data and scheduled reports |
| Support | This help center |

Need help? Click the chat bubble in the bottom-right corner to reach support.
