# Your First Week

Follow this checklist in your first five days to get comfortable with the platform.

## Day 1 — Explore the catalog

- Open **My Catalog** from the left sidebar.
- Filter by **Status: Active** to see your live products.
- Click any product to review its price history, competitors, and current rule.

## Day 2 — Review competitor tracking

- Go to **Companies → Company Explorer**.
- Search for a known competitor and open their profile.
- Check the **Price History** tab to see how their prices have moved.

## Day 3 — Check repricing rules

- Navigate to **Repricing → Auto Repricing Rules**.
- Review which products are enrolled in automated repricing.
- For each rule, verify the min/max price guardrails are correct.

## Day 4 — Set up alerts

- Open **Settings → Alerts**.
- Enable notifications for: competitor price drops, stock-out risk, and margin below threshold.
- Choose your delivery method (email, in-app, or both).

## Day 5 — Run your first report

- Go to **Reports**.
- Generate a **Margin Summary** for the past 30 days.
- Export to CSV and share with your team.

## Tips

- Bookmark the dashboard — it's your best daily starting point.
- Use the global search (⌘K) to jump to any product or competitor quickly.
- The AI assistant (chat bubble) can answer questions about specific products.
