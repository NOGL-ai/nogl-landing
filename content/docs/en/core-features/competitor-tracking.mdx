# Competitor Tracking

NOGL scrapes competitor product pages daily (and on-demand) to keep your price intelligence up to date.

## Adding a competitor

1. Go to **Companies → Tracked Competitors**.
2. Click **Add Competitor**.
3. Enter the competitor's website URL (e.g. `https://www.example-shop.de`).
4. NOGL will auto-discover their catalog. This can take up to 30 minutes for large stores.

## Matching competitor products to yours

Once a competitor is added, NOGL tries to auto-match their products to your SKUs using title similarity and barcode matching.

To review matches:
1. Open the competitor profile.
2. Go to the **Product Matches** tab.
3. Accept, reject, or manually reassign matches.

## Price history

The **Price History** tab shows a chart of the competitor's price over time for any matched product. You can compare their price to yours side-by-side.

## Scrape frequency

| Plan | Default scrape frequency |
|------|------------------------|
| Starter | Daily (midnight UTC) |
| Growth | Every 6 hours |
| Enterprise | Every hour + on-demand |

To trigger an on-demand scrape, open the competitor profile and click **Refresh Now**.

## Price change alerts

You can be notified immediately when a tracked competitor changes a price. See [Alerts](/en/fractional-cfo/docs/core-features/alerts) for configuration.

## Removing a competitor

Open the competitor profile, click **Actions → Remove**. All historical price data is retained for 12 months.
