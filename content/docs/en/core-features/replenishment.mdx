# Replenishment

The Replenishment module analyzes your current stock levels, sales velocity, and lead times to generate reorder recommendations before you run out.

## How it works

Every morning, NOGL calculates the **days of stock remaining** for each active SKU:

```
Days of stock = Current stock ÷ Average daily sales (last 14 days)
```

When days of stock fall below your **reorder point** (default: lead time + 7 days safety stock), a replenishment alert is triggered.

## Viewing recommendations

1. Go to **Catalog → Replenishment**.
2. Use the **Status** filter to show only items flagged for reorder.
3. Each row shows: current stock, daily velocity, days remaining, and suggested order quantity.

## Suggested order quantity

The suggested quantity is calculated as:

```
Suggested qty = (Lead time × Daily velocity) + Safety stock − Current stock
```

You can override the suggestion by clicking the quantity field and entering your own value.

## Bulk export

Click **Export** to download all recommendations as a CSV, ready to send to your supplier or upload to your ERP.

## Setting lead times

Lead times are set per supplier under **Settings → Suppliers**. If no supplier lead time is set, the system defaults to 14 days.

## Safety stock

The default safety stock is 7 days. You can override this per product in **Catalog → Product → Replenishment Settings**.
