# Alerts

Alerts notify you (by email or in-app) when something important happens that requires your attention.

## Types of alerts

| Alert type | Trigger |
|-----------|---------|
| **Competitor price drop** | A tracked competitor drops their price by more than X% |
| **Stock-out risk** | A product's days of stock falls below your reorder point |
| **Margin below threshold** | A product's margin drops below your set minimum |
| **Repricing blocked** | A rule could not apply because it would breach a guardrail |
| **Catalog import error** | A scheduled catalog import fails or contains validation errors |

## Setting up alerts

1. Go to **Settings → Alerts**.
2. Toggle on each alert type you want to receive.
3. For each type, set the threshold (e.g. "notify when competitor drops price > 5%").
4. Choose delivery channels: **In-app**, **Email**, or both.
5. Click **Save**.

## Per-product alert overrides

You can set different thresholds per product:

1. Open a product in **My Catalog**.
2. Scroll to the **Alerts** section.
3. Override any workspace-level threshold.

## Viewing alert history

All alerts are logged in **Settings → Alert History**. You can filter by type, date range, and product.

## Muting alerts

To temporarily mute all alerts (e.g. during a planned maintenance window):

1. Go to **Settings → Alerts**.
2. Click **Mute all** and choose a duration (1 hour, 4 hours, or 24 hours).

Individual alert types can also be toggled off at any time.

## Alert digest

Instead of individual emails per alert, you can opt for a **Daily digest** that summarizes all alerts from the previous 24 hours. Configure this in **Settings → Alerts → Delivery**.
