# Research

This repo ships with the full corpus of research that informed the 27-metric ad-creative
scoring system, the dashboard UX, and the competitive positioning.

See **[docs/research/INDEX.md](docs/research/INDEX.md)** for the tiered index.

Highlights:
- 27 per-metric competitor analyses (Smartly.io, Celtra, Flashtalking, Marpipe, AdCreative.ai, VidMob, CreativeX, Meta, Google)
- Particl dashboard UX teardown (21 screenshots analysed)
- DE-market CTR baselines + synthetic ground-truth formulas
- Pricefy reverse-engineering
- Scraper architecture for competitive intelligence
